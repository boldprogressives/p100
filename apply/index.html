    How does it work?

    PCCC staff will review all applications and the most qualified applicants will be contacted for a follow up interview. The 25 finalists will be notified no later than three weeks prior to the training. Apply as soon as possible -- the final deadline is four weeks before the training. After the training, we'll work to place you on a progressive campaign so you can put your skills to work.

    Limited travel scholarships are available to those in need.

    Apply now by filling out the form to the right. Answer each question in 250 words or less. You'll be prompted to upload a resume once you hit submit.

    If you can't make any of these sessions you can still sign up for updates to learn about future training announcements by selecting ""Interested in Future Sessions" below.

      About P100 and PCCC

      The Progressive Change Campaign Committee's training program, P100, is training hundreds of progressive campaign staff and candidates to run effective campaigns and win. We're building an army of campaigners equipped with the most recent best practices backed by hard science and years of experience running progressive campaigns.

      Paid for by the Progressive Change Campaign Committee PAC (BoldProgressives.org) and not authorized by any candidate or candidate's committee. Contributions to the PCCC are not deductible as charitable contributions for federal income tax purposes.
