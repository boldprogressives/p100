Trainings

Candidate Training

We've trained hundreds of federal, state and local candidates across the country. Our curriculum covers everything from improving fundraising, perfecting the stump speech and delivery, achieving great television appearances, hiring excellent staff, and making sure that polling is useful and tests winning messaging.

Media Training

At our media training, folks learn how to get more earned media coverage by planning great events, finding the best speakers, building relationships with reporters and bloggers, and managing press contacts through easy-to-use online tools.

Finance Training

Our finance training teaches the fundamentals of campaign finance work for any level, from local and state to federal races. Through exercises, we learn how to write a finance plan, research donors, run effective call time, file compliance reports, organize successful fundraising events and more!

Field Training

At our field training, we learn how to calculate win numbers and target voters effectively, write a field plan, write a call script, manage data, manage volunteers and more! Most importantly, we teach the best practices that we've developed over the years through our own work, and that we've learned from our allies.

Digital Training

Our digital training teaches activists how to advocate for candidates or issues effectively online. The curriculum covers soup-to-nuts digital activism, including the best strategies for email, Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, Tumblr, and more.
